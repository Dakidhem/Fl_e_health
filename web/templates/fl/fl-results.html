Federated Learning Results: Our federated learning process utilizes the collaborative efforts of hospitals and doctors to train a global model for medical health and X-ray image analysis. Through this approach, we have achieved remarkable results. During the training phase, each participating hospital or doctor trains a local model on their own patient data while keeping the data securely on their premises. The local models are then aggregated to create the global model, benefiting from the collective knowledge of all participants. We have observed impressive training accuracy, with the global model achieving an average accuracy of over 90%. The testing accuracy has demonstrated excellent performance, consistently achieving accuracy rates above 70% in the 3rd round. Additionally, we have monitored the loss reduction throughout the training iterations, observing a significant decrease in the loss function. These exceptional results reflect the power of federated learning in harnessing the collective intelligence of healthcare professionals while preserving data privacy and security. Our approach enables hospitals and doctors to leverage a shared global model that continually learns and improves, leading to enhanced diagnostic accuracy and better patient outcomes.
